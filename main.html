Manali

Manali is a charming hill station located in the state of Himachal Pradesh, India. Situated at an altitude of 2050 meters in the Beas River Valley, Manali is renowned for its scenic beauty, snow-capped mountains, and pleasant weather.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. **Solang Valley** - Known for its picturesque landscapes and adventure sports like paragliding and zorbing.
  2. **Rohtang Pass** - A high mountain pass that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks and glaciers.
  3. **Hadimba Temple** - An ancient cave temple dedicated to Hadimba Devi, built in a unique architectural style.
  4. **Manu Temple** - A revered temple dedicated to Sage Manu, located in Old Manali.
  5. **Vashisht Hot Water Springs** - Natural hot springs with medicinal properties, ideal for relaxation.

Must-Try Foods

  • **Sidu** - A local bread made from wheat flour, best enjoyed with ghee.
  • **Thenthuk** - A hearty noodle soup that is perfect for the cold weather.
  • **Dham** - A traditional Himachali meal consisting of rice, dal, rajma, curd, and more, served during festivals and special occasions.
  • **Kadhi Chawal** - A comforting dish of yogurt-based curry served with rice.
  • **Sepu Badi** - Lentil dumplings cooked in a flavorful gravy, a specialty of the region.
